# UX audit and design strategy

## Current issues
- The interface used plain white surfaces and minimal spacing, which made the product feel unfinished.
- Navigation and dashboard content lacked a clear information hierarchy or sense of progress.
- The experience needed a stronger school ERP identity, richer cards, and clearer action patterns.

## Design system applied
- A polished blue-purple palette with soft gradients and layered cards.
- Type hierarchy tuned for calm, readable dashboards and elevated call-to-action buttons.
- A reusable card, shell, and component language for consistent SaaS-style experiences.
- A streamlined dashboard layout that prioritizes progress, focus, and next actions.
